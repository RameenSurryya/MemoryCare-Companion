About MemoryCare

Designed to make daily care simpler, safer, and calmer.

MemoryCare Companion is a web-based support system for people who need help following daily medication and routine tasks. It helps users stay organized while giving caregivers a clear way to monitor missed or completed actions.

Why this system matters

Dementia can make everyday routines confusing, especially when tasks need to happen at the right time. Missing medication, forgetting meals, or losing track of simple daily actions can affect safety and confidence.

This project focuses on a gentle and easy-to-use experience. The interface uses calm colors, clear sections, large readable text, and simple actions so users do not feel overwhelmed.

For Users

Users can view their daily tasks and mark important actions as completed with a simple and friendly dashboard.

For Caregivers

Caregivers can monitor activity, check missed routines, and support users before small issues become bigger concerns.

For Admins

Admins can manage accounts, control access, update roles, and keep the system organized and secure.
